How this prototype works

Your selected symptoms are converted into a structured feature vector and passed to a trained machine learning model. The model predicts a likely condition and maps it to an appointment urgency level.

Urgency levels

Emergency Immediate or same‑day care recommended.
Urgent Needs prompt assessment but not life‑threatening.
Routine Suitable for a standard scheduled appointment.

This tool is a proof‑of‑concept built with synthetic data for a university project. It does not provide real medical advice. Always seek a qualified healthcare professional.
